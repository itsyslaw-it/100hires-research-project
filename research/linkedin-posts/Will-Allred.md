## Latest Post (Scraped)

Will pushes back on simplistic AI hot takes and shares a more detailed framework for how AI changes work over time.

Key points from the post:
- He outlines a progression from workflow automation and dashboards to copilots, agent workflows, agentic solutions, and eventually agentic platforms.
- He argues the transition is gradual, with significant gray areas between stages.
- He notes humans currently remain involved in setup, guidance, and oversight, but intervention can decrease as systems mature.
- He emphasizes that deep domain expertise is a defensible advantage when building effective AI products.

Main takeaway:
AI adoption is not a single jump; it evolves through stages, and the practical impact on roles depends on how autonomous systems become in real workflows.
