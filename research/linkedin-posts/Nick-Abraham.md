## Latest Post (Scraped)

Everyone in outbound wants to talk about signals, but one valuable signal is often overlooked.

Instead of:
- scanning competitor social likes
- emailing website visitors
- reaching out after recent funding

The suggested approach is:
1. Monitor freelance job boards (Upwork, Fiverr, etc.) for jobs related to your service.
2. Identify the job poster and find their email.
3. Reach out by referencing the job post, then transition to a pitch.

Why this signal can work:
- A company posting on freelance boards usually has urgent demand.
- It reflects active intent, often stronger than many common outbound signals.
- Similar signal-based outreach already works with traditional job postings.

Main challenge:
- Freelance platforms often anonymize employers, making attribution and contact discovery harder.
