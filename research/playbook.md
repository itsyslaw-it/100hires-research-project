# Cold Outreach Pipeline for B2B SaaS – Playbook

## 1. Overview
This playbook outlines a practical cold outreach system for B2B SaaS companies, based on insights from active practitioners in outbound sales. It is designed for early-stage teams or individuals looking to generate pipeline through cold email and LinkedIn outreach.

---
## 2. Where experts disagree

### 1. Activity Scale vs Message Quality

- Kyle Coleman highlights a large activity lift, from 35 calls/rep/day to 150, driven by infrastructure, tooling, and process design  
(source: Kyle Coleman, LinkedIn, June 2026 – https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7424877366570610688/)

- Josh Braun argues outreach effectiveness starts with clarity, relevance, and empathy in the opener (including transcript-first contexts)  
(source: Josh Braun, LinkedIn, June 2026 – https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7338632537465896961/)

My decision:  
I side more with Josh Braun on sequencing. Activity and systems matter, but weak messaging quality compounds wasted effort.
