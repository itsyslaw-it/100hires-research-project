## Latest Post (Scraped)

Some days I feel like quitting. Some days I want to pop off on people talking crazy online. Then I remember one quote from MLK.

"Darkness cannot drive out darkness; only light can do that. Hate cannot drive out hate; only love can do that."

I feel like every other day something more insane is happening in the world. People talking about AI taking over the world. More people seem to be going through hard times. I sometimes wonder if it is ever going to get better. Then I realize we can create life how we want.

Every morning I wake up and tell myself: "When I enter a room, I bring life and light."

Earlier in my career though I was driven by other emotions. I have to prove these people wrong. I will show them all. No one believes in me so it's up to me. I believe everyone has seasons where they need certain things to fuel them. That was legacy Morgan. This is Morgan now.

I tap into the light that people can follow. There's a better fuel that leads to success. I want to win by showing love, not by feeding into the darkness.

Sure, I still make A LOT of mistakes on a day to day basis. But I know if I focus on the light, it will be hard for me to lose.

I know MLK did not speak for my rights for me to operate in anger. He would want me to operate in pure light.

So I've been doing the work with my coach to bring light and love instead of feeding my inner darkness. Because I would rather be remembered for the light I bring. Instead of fueling the darkness I can easily fall into.

P.S. How are you reflecting on MLK's words?
